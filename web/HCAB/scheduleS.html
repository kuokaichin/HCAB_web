Scheduling

This semester we have four fantastic opportunities to meet with professionals in geriatric care and Alzheimer’s disease. Because all of our guests are taking time out from their day to meet with us, we need a strong showing at each event. We hope you will enjoy these chances to ask questions, get answers, and hear more about what affects our buddies.

Please check all the dates that you will be attending. The first and last events are mandatory and you may choose one of the middle two. You are more than welcome to attend both of these though!


Dr. Nancy Donovan about our experiences with our buddies. Our cases and challenges will described and discussed with Dr. Donovan providing insight into the underlying psychological issues our buddies may have. She is interested in and has studied apathy and depression and this will be a great opportunity for all of us to talk, not just listen.
Wednesday October 17, 7:15 PM
Adams Upper Common Room (MANDATORY)


Dr. Tony Weiner is a geriatric psychiatrist at Mass General Hospital and after a brief chat over dinner he will present his talk, “Non-pharmacological Interventions to Reduce Agitation in Nursing Home Settings.”
Monday October 29, 6 PM
Lowell Junior Common Room


Mrs. Debra Katt-Lloyd is the director of the Alzheimer’s Organization for New England. She is coming for dinner to talk about patient anecdotes, family needs, and activism to increase Alzheimer’s Disease awareness.
Tuesday November 13, 6 PM
Lowell Junior Common Room


Mrs. Robin Stewart is the Hebrew Senior Life, Life Enhancement Coordinator. She will providing us with feedback from families and some news about our buddies changes.
Sunday December 2, 1 PM
HSL(MANDATORY)
